Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tota Industry handles information when you visit our website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

Information Collection

Tota Industry is a fully static website. We do not run a backend server, database, or user account system. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. Your theme preference and popup dismissal state are stored locally in your browser via Local Storage and Session Storage — this data never leaves your device.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. We rely only on browser Local Storage to remember your selected theme and to avoid showing the welcome popup repeatedly within the same session. No advertising or analytics cookies are set.

Third-Party Services

Our sidebar and floating buttons link to external platforms such as WhatsApp, Telegram and Instagram. These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data once you leave our site. We encourage you to review their policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms.

Cloudflare R2 Hosted Videos

All video content on Tota Industry is hosted on Cloudflare R2. When you play or download a video, your browser connects directly to Cloudflare's infrastructure. Cloudflare may process technical request data (such as IP address and timestamps) as part of its content delivery service, in accordance with its own privacy policy. We do not control or have access to that data.

External Links

This site contains links to WhatsApp, Telegram and Instagram. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external websites. Visiting an external link is your choice, and we recommend reviewing their terms and privacy policies.

Copyright Notice

All video content is hosted on Cloudflare R2 and is provided for streaming and download. If you believe any content hosted on this site infringes your copyright, please contact us through our official channels so we can review and take appropriate action promptly.
